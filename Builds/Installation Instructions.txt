This mod only works with the 1.02 modules. For questions, contact Boothand at https://jk2.info

JK2MV (installed):
Place the Birdbones pk3 file in your Documents/jk2mv/base folder.

JK2MV (portable) or JK2MP (not recommended):
Place the Birdbones pk3 file in your JK2's installation directory's base folder.

As usual, make sure it doesn't conflict with other cgame mods or previous versions.
